Basic 2-Image Templates

Using Template Files

  1. Download the .txt template you like.
  2. Rename it to template.txt and place it in your mod’s template/config folder.

Templates marked as “gradient” are more complex and may have a small warning:
“Excessive template gradient may cause disconnects”.

Want your template featured? Open a ticket or send in templates in the presets channel.
