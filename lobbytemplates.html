Lobby Templates

Premade templates included: Welcome, Rules, OnMeeting, Discord, VIP.

To use a template:

  1. Rename the file to template.dat
  2. Edit the text to match your lobby’s style
  3. Remove colortag: inside the file as you customize it

Basic Templates

These only include Welcome, Rules, Onmeeting

Using Template Files

  1. Download the .txt template you like.
  2. Rename it to template.txt and place it in your mod’s template/config folder.

Templates marked as “gradient” are more complex and may have a small warning:
“Excessive template gradient may cause disconnects”.
